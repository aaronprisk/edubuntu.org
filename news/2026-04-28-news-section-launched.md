---
title: News Section Is Live
date: 2026-04-28
summary: Edubuntu.org now supports markdown-powered news updates.
---

We just launched a markdown-powered news workflow on the Edubuntu homepage.

To publish a new update:

1. Add a new markdown file in the `news` directory.
2. Add that filename to `news/index.json`.
3. Reload the page.

The homepage will automatically load and render your post.
